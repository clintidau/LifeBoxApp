About

Have you ever wished you could remember what was said in a forgotten conversation? Do you need a simple way to record meetings at work, or lectures at school? Would you like to hear yourself snoring at night?

LifeBoxApp is a Lifelogging and Sousveillance app for your Android device that captures audio 24/7 and (optionally) uploads it to your personal Dropbox account.

LifeBoxPlayer is a Java application for Windows/Linux/Mac that helps you to review your recordings.

Security

Your recordings are protected with SSL during transmission to and from Dropbox. You can set a password to encrypt recordings with AES before they are uploaded. However, recordings stored on your Android device or on your PC are not encrypted. You should always enable device encryption.

Legality

Please check your local laws before using LifeBoxApp. As a general rule, you should always ask permission before recording other people.

Privacy Policy

We do not collect any data other than the anonymous statistics collected by the Google Play Store and GitHub. If you choose to sign in to Dropbox you should read the Dropbox privacy policy.
